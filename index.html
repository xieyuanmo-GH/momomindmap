ACT FOR CLIMATE CHANGE BOARD GAME

Board Game · Community Workshops · 2019–Now

As climate change intensifies, communities need new ways to discuss and imagine responses together. I designed a collaborative board game that combines storytelling, systems thinking and playful prompts to help residents explore actions they can take in their daily lives, while strengthening local support networks.

Co-creation · Community Engagement · Climate Action

TRASH FL!P CARD GAME

Gamified Workshops · 2019–Now

Trash FL!P is a fast-paced card game and workshop format developed to rethink waste sorting in China. Through playful missions and storytelling, participants discover where their trash goes, what systems are behind it, and how they can shift everyday habits. The game has been used in schools, NGOs and public events.

Game Design · Environmental Education · Workshop Design

WASTE MANAGEMENT INFOGRAPHIC

Infographic · Research Collaboration · 2018–2020

To better understand waste management in China, I collaborated with NGOs and researchers to map the journey of waste from households to treatment facilities. The result is an illustrated infographic used in exhibitions, public talks and school workshops to spark conversations about circular systems.

Systems Mapping · Infographic · Public Engagement

REDESIGN UNFINISHED BUILDINGS

Master Thesis · 2015

This master's thesis looks at a “ghost city” project in Tianjin and asks what could happen if speculative real estate were re-occupied by those who need housing the most. The proposal turns an unfinished high-rise into a vertical farm combined with affordable housing for migrant workers.

Ghost Cities · Adaptive Reuse · Vertical Farming

CAPSULE WAY OF LIVING

Undergraduate Thesis · 2013

Exploring architecture as a product, this project imagines modular capsule units that can be stacked and rearranged like building blocks. It is both a proposal for compact living in megacities and a metaphor for how people negotiate personal space under pressure.

Compact Living · Modular Design · Speculative Architecture
